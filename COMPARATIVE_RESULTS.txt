ANALYSIS:
1. Performance: Hybrid encryption prioritizes ROI using CNN detection. 
   While detection adds overhead on small images, it allows significantly
   higher throughput on large high-resolution datasets.
2. Security (Entropy): Both methods achieve near-theoretical maximum entropy
   (8.0 bits), which is crucial for resisting frequency analysis.
3. Diffusion (Avalanche): Both maintain the necessary property where a single
   input bit change flips approx. 50% of the bits in the corresponding block.
